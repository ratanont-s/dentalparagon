Aesthetic Denture

Lots of individuals lose their confidence to smile when they lose their teeth. If you think losing a tooth is not a big deal, just ask someone who has lost a tooth. Most individuals experience a loss of self esteem and self confidence when they are missing teeth. Dentures are the conventional method used to replace missing teeth and consist of prosthetic false teeth which are created to replace the missing.

Dentures are supported by the surrounding tissue in the oral cavity and may be removable or may rely on bonding or dental implants. On the occasion that some teeth are still present, it is possible to combine the denture with some dental crowns and or similar attachments to enable invisible clasp retention.

Full Set Denture Soluion

Our Dentists can provide a FULL SET of natural-looking dentures for patients who have lost all or most of their teeth. This Dentures Solution does not only improve smiles, but more importantly, it will improve live style and confidence of your self.

Partial Denture Solution

partial dentures can be either fixed or removable, and serves to replace the missing teeth and prevent movement of neighboring teeth.

Over-dentures

these are supported with implants and can be removed and reinserted by the patient. It increases bite force and can give the patient more facial contour, for a more pleasing profile.

Dentures have come along way and thanks to advancements in technology individuals can now enjoy more aesthetically pleasing dentures which compliment their appearance. Aesthetic dentures must be made to look and feel as natural and comfortable as normal healthy teeth. The overall comfort of your aesthetic dentures is extremely important and there are several factors which may contribute to the overall fit of your aesthetic dentures. Some of these factors include the condition of the tissue and bone structure in your oral cavity as well as the shape and size of your mouth. While most conventional aesthetic dentures could not be customized, it is now possible to obtain aesthetic dentures which are based on the individuals face dimensions and features to enhance the individual’s appearance, and thus enable the individual to have aesthetic dentures which are as close to their natural teeth as possible.
