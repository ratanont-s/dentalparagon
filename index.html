Dental Implant

Dental implant is considered as the best solution of tooth replacement available at the present. Dental Implants are made of Titanium, a stable, totally resistant metal that is biocompatible and therefore well tolerated by the human body.

Dental Implants are placed directly into the jawbone and act as replacement roots upon which crowns and bridges may be fixed. Dental implants offer a permanent fixed tooth replacement. Not only the appearance, but also the safe feeling of natural teeth is restored

TEETH WHITENING
